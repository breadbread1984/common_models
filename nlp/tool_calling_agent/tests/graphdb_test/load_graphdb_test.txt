Elon Musk, the CEO of Tesla and SpaceX, announced during a press conference in California that SpaceX would be launching its next-generation rocket, Starship, from Cape Canaveral in Florida. Tesla, based in Palo Alto, has also been developing AI technologies for its autonomous vehicles, a project led by Andrej Karpathy. NASA and SpaceX have been collaborating on various space missions, including the Crew Dragon program, which successfully transported astronauts to the International Space Station (ISS) in 2020.
